Hello, it's Ariel!

I am a third-year undergraduate student pursuing dual degrees in Information (UX focus) and Psychology at University of Michigan.

Coming from a psychology background, I have always been curious about human motivation and behavior. This has given me a great foundation when empathizing with users and understanding their contexts.

UX design allows me to explore a wide range of design tools and to rediscover my creative energy.

My extracurricular activities have a heavy focus on social justice, community engagement, and social work. UX gives me the tool to embody my values in my work in a fresh, new approach.

In my free time, I like to dance, book, bake, and volunteer at my local animal shelter (to play with cats!)
